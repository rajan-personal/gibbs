Common Questions

FAQ

Are your clutch springs compatible with Tata and Ashok Leyland engines?+
Yes. We manufacture clutch and engine springs compatible with all major Tata and Ashok Leyland engine series including BS4 and BS6 platforms.
What is shot peening and why is it important for engine springs?+
Shot peening is a surface treatment that induces compressive residual stress in the spring surface, significantly improving fatigue life — critical for high-cycle engine springs.
Do you supply valve springs for Cummins diesel engines?+
Yes. We manufacture valve springs compatible with Cummins B, C, L, and ISB series diesel engines. Send us the engine model for exact specifications.
Can I order a sample batch before placing a bulk order?+
Yes. We welcome trial orders so you can verify quality and fitment before committing to bulk quantities.
